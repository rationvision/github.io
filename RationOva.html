RationOva

Automatic Counting and Quality control of Salmon ova

AI for Precision Counting and Inspection

Accurate counting and quality inspection of salmon ovas is essential to reduce costs and optimize farming 

Fast and accurate counting and quality inspection of salmon ova are crucial for producers and smolt farms. With the global scale-up of salmon farming, manual egg counting is impractical, and inaccuracies in volume-based methods can lead to suboptimal smolt tank utilization. RationOva addresses this by using computer vision to count, measure, and detect defective eggs on a conveyor belt in real-time, ensuring efficiency and precision. 

Key Features

State-of-the-art computer vision

Automatic counting, size estimation, and quality inspection.

Cloud-services

Image analysis results and statistics are stored in the cloud for long-term traceability

User interface

User controls and real-time ova counting and image analysis results are visible on site
